Ninja Warrior

Ninja warrior originated from a Japanese sports variety show, Sasuke.
Every competition challenge the competitor against a four stage obstacle course. Each stage increasing in obstacle difficulty. Only completing of one stage then is the competitor allow to advance to the next.


Ever since Sasuke aired on television in 2002,
the sport has since increased its popularity around the world with different country having their own version of the competition.
