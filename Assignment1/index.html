SG Ninja Warrior: Alvin Tan

Alvin is Singapore’s top ninja warrior competitor. He represents his country in ninja competitions around the world.

Determined to create awareness and guidance on the sport. Alvin started to organise group training sessions at playgrounds and parks. Currently he coaches at Singapore’s first and only ninja warrior gym, Ninja SG.

Competition highlights:

  • Sasuke Singapore stage 2 (Singapore, 2012)
  • Sasuke ASEAN Cup (Malaysia, 2014)
  • Sasuke Vietnam stage 3 (Vietnam, 2016)
  • Tough Warrior finals (Thailand, 2016)
  • Velocity Urban Attack finals (Singapore, 2017)
  • Asia OCR Championship OCR 100m 2nd place (Philippines, 2019)
  • Ninja Challenge League finals stage 2 (Australia, 2019)
